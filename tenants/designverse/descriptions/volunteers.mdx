# DesignVerse 2026 - Volunteer Registration

Welcome to DesignVerse 2026. Thank you for considering becoming a volunteer, we appreciate your efforts to help support DesignVerse 2026.

## **Event Information**

**DesignVerse 2026** is a designathon spanning 24 hours through **May 16–17, 2026.**

Volunteers are not required to stay the full duration of the event, but are encouraged to check out the various events, workshops, and opportunities that are available.

Volunteer duties include but are not limited to setup, workshop organization, providing expertise/assistance to designer, food handling, cleanup and more.

Note: Volunteers are not permitted to become participants for the designathon.
