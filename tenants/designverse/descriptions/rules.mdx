## General Rules

1. **Teams:** Teams must consist of 2-4 participants.
2. **Ownership:** All code, design, and content must be created during the hackathon.
3. **Respect:** Be respectful to all participants, volunteers, judges, and sponsors.
4. **Submissions:** All projects must be submitted to Devpost by the deadline.
5. **Demo:** Teams must present their project to the judges during the judging period.

## Eligibility

- Open to currently enrolled university students and recent graduates (within 12 months).
- Participants must be present at the venue for the full event unless pre-approved.

## Judging Criteria

1. **Innovation & Creativity** — How unique and original is the idea?
2. **Technical Execution** — Difficulty and quality of the technical implementation.
3. **Impact & Relevance** — Does it solve a real problem or meet the hackathon track goals?
4. **Presentation & Demo** — How well is the project communicated?
