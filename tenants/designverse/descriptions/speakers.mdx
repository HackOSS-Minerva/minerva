## **Event Information**

**DesignVerse 2026** is a designathon spanning 24 hours through **May 16–17, 2026.**

Speakers will have the opportunity to present their expertise and insights to our participants during the event. This is a great opportunity to share knowledge and inspire the next generation of designers.

Note: Speakers are not permitted to become participants for the designathon.
