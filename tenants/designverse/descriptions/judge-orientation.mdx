## 🧑‍⚖️ Judge Orientation

### **DesignVerse 2026 — Judging Guidelines**

Thank you for serving as a judge. Your role is essential to making this event a success. Please review the following guidelines before the event begins.

---

### 📋 Review Rubrics Beforehand
Familiarize yourself with the judging criteria: Innovation, Technical Complexity, Presentation, Impact, and Design.

### ⏰ Arrive on Time
Join your assigned judging slots 5 minutes early to ensure a smooth transition between teams.

### 💬 Be Constructive
Provide actionable feedback that helps teams improve. Focus on strengths and areas for growth.

### ⚖️ Stay Objective
Evaluate each project fairly against the rubric. Avoid bias based on team size or prior knowledge.

---

### 🆘 Questions?
If you have any questions, reach out to the organizing team on Discord or email us at designverseucr@gmail.com.
