## Our Commitment

DesignVerse is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all participants. We value the participation of every individual and want everyone to have a positive experience.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- **Be respectful** — Different viewpoints and experiences are welcome.
- **Be collaborative** — Work together and help others where you can.
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome new ideas and perspectives.
- **Be kind** — Treat everyone with empathy and consideration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated. This includes:

- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, body size, race, or religion.
- Inappropriate language, imagery, or sexual attention.
- Bullying, intimidation, or unwelcome attention.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to event organizers immediately. You can reach us on Discord or at the event info desk.

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Anyone asked to stop such behavior is expected to comply immediately. Event organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including expulsion from the event without warning or refund.
