# DesignVerse 2026 - Project Submission

Submit your project for judging. Please fill out your team information and provide links to your deliverables.

## **Guidelines**

- You must submit a **Team Name**, **Project Name**, and **Devpost URL**.
- You must provide at least one link to **GitHub**, **Figma**, or **Canva**.
- You may optionally invite other members to the submission by their email addresses.
